Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Milk (as needed to make dough)
  • Oil or ghee for frying
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp rose water
  • Cardamom pods (optional)

Procedure

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, and ghee. Add milk gradually to form a soft dough.
  2. Rest the dough for 10 minutes, then shape into smooth balls.
  3. Heat oil on low flame and fry the balls until golden brown. Drain and set aside.
  4. Prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar, water, and cardamom for 10 minutes. Add rose water.
  5. Soak fried balls in warm syrup for at least 1 hour.
  6. Serve warm or chilled.
