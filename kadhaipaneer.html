Kadhai Paneer

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1 large green bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds (crushed)
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  1. Heat oil in a kadhai (wok). Add cumin and crushed coriander seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add onions and sauté until translucent. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add tomato puree, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Add bell peppers and cook for 3–4 minutes until slightly tender.
  5. Add paneer cubes, garam masala, and kasuri methi. Mix gently and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or roti.
