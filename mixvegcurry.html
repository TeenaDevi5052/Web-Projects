Mix Veg Curry

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup carrots, diced
  • 1/2 cup beans, chopped
  • 1/2 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 potato, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  1. Boil or steam all vegetables until just tender. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add onions and sauté until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add tomato puree, turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  5. Add the boiled vegetables and mix well. Simmer for 5–7 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  7. Serve hot with rice, roti, or naan.
