Masala Dosa

Ingredients

  • For Dosa Batter:
  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed for grinding and fermenting)
  • Oil for cooking dosas
  • For Potato Masala:
  • 2 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Few curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds for 6 hours. Grind to a smooth batter and ferment overnight.
  2. Add salt to the fermented batter and mix well.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chili. Sauté onions until soft.
  4. Add turmeric and mashed potatoes. Mix well and cook for 2–3 minutes. Garnish with coriander leaves.
  5. Heat a dosa tawa. Pour a ladle of batter and spread into a thin circle. Drizzle oil around edges.
  6. Cook until crisp and golden. Place a portion of potato masala in the center and fold the dosa.
  7. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
