Ladoo

Ingredients

  • 2 cups besan (gram flour)
  • 3/4 cup ghee
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 2 tbsp chopped nuts (cashews, almonds)
  • Raisins (optional)

Procedure

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and add besan. Roast on low flame until it turns golden and aromatic (about 15–20 minutes).
  2. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  3. Add powdered sugar, cardamom powder, and chopped nuts. Mix well.
  4. Once the mixture is cool enough to handle, shape into small round ladoos.
  5. Garnish with raisins if desired. Let them set for 30 minutes.
  6. Store in an airtight container and enjoy!
