Kulfi

Ingredients

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp chopped pistachios
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • A pinch of saffron soaked in warm milk

Procedure

  1. Boil milk and reduce it to half while stirring continuously.
  2. Add sugar, cardamom powder, saffron milk, and pistachios.
  3. Simmer for 5 more minutes and let it cool.
  4. Pour into molds or cups and freeze for 6–8 hours.
  5. Unmold and serve chilled.
