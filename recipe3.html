Palak Paneer

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 cups spinach leaves (washed)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 green chili
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp cream (optional)

Procedure

  1. Blanch spinach leaves in hot water for 2 minutes. Drain and blend with green chili into a smooth purée.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and sauté chopped onions until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and tomatoes. Cook until soft.
  4. Mix in the spinach purée and cook for 5 minutes. Add garam masala and salt.
  5. Add paneer cubes and simmer for 5–7 minutes. Stir in cream if using.
  6. Serve hot with roti, naan, or rice.
