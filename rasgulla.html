Rasgulla

Ingredients

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice or vinegar
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tsp rose water or cardamom powder (optional)

Procedure

  1. Boil milk in a pan. Once it comes to a boil, add lemon juice or vinegar to curdle it.
  2. Strain the curdled milk using a muslin cloth. Rinse with cold water to remove sourness.
  3. Hang the cloth for 30 minutes to drain excess water. Knead the chenna until smooth and soft.
  4. Divide into small balls and roll them smooth without cracks.
  5. In a wide pan, boil sugar and water to make syrup. Add rose water or cardamom if desired.
  6. Gently add the chenna balls to the boiling syrup. Cover and cook on medium flame for 15–20 minutes.
  7. Rasgullas will puff up. Let them cool in the syrup before serving.
