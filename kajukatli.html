Kaju Katli

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cashews (kaju)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder (optional)
  • 1 tsp ghee (for greasing)
  • Silver leaf (varak) for garnish (optional)

Procedure

  1. Grind cashews into a fine powder using a dry grinder. Do not over-grind or it may release oil.
  2. In a pan, combine sugar and water. Heat until it forms a one-string consistency syrup.
  3. Lower the flame and add cashew powder gradually, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Cook the mixture until it thickens and starts leaving the sides of the pan.
  5. Add cardamom powder if using. Mix well and turn off the heat.
  6. Transfer the mixture onto a greased surface. Let it cool slightly, then knead into a smooth dough.
  7. Roll out the dough into a thin layer and apply silver leaf if desired.
  8. Cut into diamond-shaped pieces and serve or store in an airtight container.
