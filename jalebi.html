Jalebi

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp corn flour
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • Water (as needed to make batter)
  • Oil or ghee for frying
  • For Sugar Syrup:
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp saffron strands (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/2 tsp lemon juice

Procedure

  1. Mix flour, corn flour, yogurt, and baking soda. Add water gradually to make a smooth, flowing batter.
  2. Cover and let it ferment for 8–10 hours or overnight.
  3. Prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar and water until slightly sticky. Add saffron, cardamom, and lemon juice. Keep warm.
  4. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Pour batter into a piping bag or squeeze bottle.
  5. Pipe spiral shapes into hot oil and fry until golden and crisp.
  6. Immediately transfer fried jalebis into warm sugar syrup. Soak for 1–2 minutes.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
