Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato)
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Saffron soaked in warm milk (optional)

Procedure

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes. Cook until 90% done and set aside.
  2. Heat oil/ghee in a pan. Add whole spices and sauté sliced onions until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and chopped tomatoes. Cook until soft.
  4. Mix in vegetables, yogurt, biryani masala, and salt. Cook until veggies are tender.
  5. Layer cooked rice over the vegetable mixture. Sprinkle saffron milk, mint, and coriander.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 10–15 minutes (dum method).
  7. Fluff gently and serve hot with raita or salan.
