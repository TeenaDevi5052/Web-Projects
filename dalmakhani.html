Dal Makhani

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup whole black urad dal (black gram)
  • 2 tbsp rajma (kidney beans)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  1. Soak urad dal and rajma overnight. Pressure cook with salt until soft (about 6–8 whistles).
  2. Heat oil and butter in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add onions and sauté until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add tomato puree, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  5. Add cooked dal and rajma. Mix well and simmer on low flame for 30–40 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add cream and garam masala. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and a dollop of butter. Serve hot with naan or rice.
