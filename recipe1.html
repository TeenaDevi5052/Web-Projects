Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken (cut into cubes)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato purée
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Procedure

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, and salt. Refrigerate for 1 hour.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and cook the marinated chicken until lightly browned and cooked through. Set aside.
  3. In another pan, melt butter and add tomato purée. Cook for 5–7 minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add cream, garam masala, and kasuri methi. Stir well and simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Add the cooked chicken to the gravy and mix thoroughly. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or basmati rice.
