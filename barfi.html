Barfi

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts for garnish (almonds, pistachios)
  • Saffron strands (optional)

Procedure

  1. Heat ghee in a non-stick pan. Add milk and sugar, stir until sugar dissolves.
  2. Add milk powder gradually, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  3. Cook on low flame until the mixture thickens and starts leaving the sides of the pan.
  4. Add cardamom powder and saffron strands. Mix well.
  5. Transfer the mixture to a greased tray and flatten evenly.
  6. Garnish with chopped nuts and let it cool completely.
  7. Cut into squares and serve or store in an airtight container.
